Move Tabs

  • Left: alt + shift + [
  • Right: alt + shift + ]
  • To New Window: alt + m
  • To New Window (Same Domains): alt + shift + m
  • To One Window: alt + e
  • To Position: alt + {1-9}

Other Actions

  • Close To the Right: alt + w
  • Pin: alt + p
  • Duplicate: alt + d
  • Refresh All: alt + r
  • Highlight Left: alt + shift + left
  • Highlight Right: alt + shift + right
